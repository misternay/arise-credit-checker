⚠️ UX Issues Identified

  • Extreme Hover Friction: Hovering "Accounts" → "Work Account" → "Info Submenu" is highly error-prone.
  • No Inline Overview: You cannot see status details at a glance without opening multiple submenus.
  • Unpolished Aesthetics: Emoji icons (🟢, 🟡) do not feel integrated with macOS design language.
Proposed Redesign

Flat 2-Level Menu

Accounts sit directly in the root menu for instant viewing. Each account gets its own single submenu for actions and info details.
